Shipping Information

Whether your order is for one book or one hundred, whether you live in the Ukraine or overseas, we deliver directly to your home, or work-place at no charge.
Delivery times vary from region to region and, within that, from country to country. Our average Standard delivery times are as follows:


Europe 5-7 days
Africa & the Middle East 10-14 days
Asia-Pacific 14-21 days
Latin America & the Caribbean 14-21 days
North America Not a region serviced by Archive Bookstore

Expedited Shipping

For customers wishing to receive orders by priority dispatch, an expedited service is available. Here again, we are able to ship to all parts of the world*. Once In our Shopping Cart a shipping cost is automatically calculated based on the total weight of the products(s) ordered. For your convenience and peace of mind the total cost of dispatch is displayed at the checkout before you confirm your order.
Please note, that expedited delivery times vary from region to region and, within that, from country to country. Once your order has been dispatched we will contact you within email.

Import Taxes/Custom Duties

Orders dispatched to countries outside the UK may be subject to import taxes, customs duties and fees levied by the destination country. Customs policies vary widely from country to country and you are advised to contact your local Customs and Excise office for further information. The recipient may be subject to such import taxes, customs duties and fees, which are, typically, charged once a shipment reaches the destination country, to include additional customs clearance charges. Eurospan has no control over import taxes and/or custom duties and can neither predict what they may be, nor be liable for them if they are levied.
